Daltons AI B.V. is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Dutch UAVG, and applicable international privacy laws including CCPA (California), PDPA (Singapore), PDPL (UAE), APPI (Japan) and PDPO (Hong Kong).

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, company name
  • Account information: login credentials, subscription plan, billing details
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, session duration, IP address
  • Payment data: processed by Stripe — we do not store card details
  • Communication data: emails, chat messages, support requests
  • Lead data: business contact information sourced from public databases on behalf of clients

3. Why We Process Your Data

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • Contract performance: To deliver the services you subscribed to
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our platform, send service notifications, and prevent fraud
  • Legal obligation: For tax records, financial reporting, and compliance with Dutch law
  • Consent: For marketing communications (you can opt out at any time)

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active, plus:

  • Financial records: 7 years (Dutch tax law requirement)
  • Account data: 2 years after account closure
  • Marketing data: until you unsubscribe
  • Lead data: 12 months or until deletion requested

5. Who We Share Data With

We use the following third-party processors under data processing agreements:

  • Stripe Inc. — Payment processing (USA, Privacy Shield certified)
  • Vercel Inc. — Website hosting (USA)

All transfers outside the EU/EEA are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as required by GDPR Article 46.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable international laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Withdraw consent: At any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any right, email privacy@daltonsai.com. We respond within 30 days.

7. International Markets — Additional Rights

California (CCPA): You have the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal data. Contact us to request data deletion.

Singapore (PDPA): You may withdraw consent for collection, use or disclosure of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

UAE (PDPL): We comply with Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection. Sensitive data is not transferred outside the UAE without appropriate safeguards.

Japan (APPI): Personal information is handled in accordance with Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information.

Hong Kong (PDPO): We comply with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance Cap. 486.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — required for the platform to function. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies without your explicit consent. You can manage cookie preferences via our cookie banner.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS), access controls, regular security assessments, and employee training on data protection.

10. Data Breach Notification
