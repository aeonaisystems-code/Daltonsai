Data Processing Agreement

Last updated: March 11, 2026 · Daltons AI B.V. · KVK 97688835

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the agreement between Daltons AI B.V. ("Processor") and the Client ("Controller") and governs the processing of personal data by Daltons AI on behalf of the Client. This DPA is required under GDPR Article 28 and applies automatically to all Enterprise plan subscriptions.

1. Definitions

"Controller" means the Client who determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.

"Processor" means Daltons AI B.V., which processes personal data on behalf of the Controller.

"Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

"Processing" means any operation performed on personal data including collection, storage, use, disclosure or deletion.

"Sub-processor" means any third party engaged by the Processor to process personal data.

2. Subject Matter & Duration

This DPA applies to the processing of personal data by Daltons AI in connection with the services described in the Terms of Service. It remains in force for the duration of the subscription and for as long as Daltons AI holds personal data on behalf of the Controller.

4. Processor Obligations

Daltons AI B.V. agrees to:

  • Process personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller
  • Ensure persons authorized to process the data are bound by confidentiality
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures per GDPR Article 32
  • Assist the Controller in fulfilling data subject requests (access, erasure, portability)
  • Delete or return all personal data upon termination of the agreement
  • Make available all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with GDPR Article 28
  • Notify the Controller without undue delay of any personal data breach

5. Controller Obligations

The Controller agrees to:

  • Ensure there is a lawful basis for all personal data provided to Daltons AI
  • Provide accurate and complete instructions for data processing
  • Notify Daltons AI promptly of any data subject requests or regulatory inquiries
  • Comply with applicable data protection laws in their jurisdiction

7. International Data Transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), Daltons AI ensures appropriate safeguards are in place including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Supplementary measures where required by the EDPB recommendations

8. Security Measures

Daltons AI implements the following technical and organizational measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption of data at rest using AES-256
  • Access controls with role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures with defined escalation paths
  • Employee training on data protection and security

9. Data Subject Rights Assistance

Daltons AI will assist the Controller in responding to data subject requests by:

  • Providing tools to export, correct or delete personal data within 5 business days of request
  • Forwarding any direct data subject requests received to the Controller immediately
  • Documenting all data subject requests and actions taken

10. Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, Daltons AI will notify the Controller within 48 hours of becoming aware. The notification will include:

  • Nature of the breach and categories of data affected
  • Approximate number of data subjects affected
  • Likely consequences of the breach
  • Measures taken or proposed to address the breach

11. Audit Rights

The Controller has the right to conduct audits or inspections of Daltons AI's data processing activities upon 30 days written notice. Daltons AI may satisfy this requirement by providing current third-party audit reports (ISO 27001, SOC 2) where available.

12. Governing Law

This DPA is governed by Dutch law. The competent courts of Amsterdam have exclusive jurisdiction over any disputes arising from this DPA.

13. Agreement

By subscribing to Daltons AI services, the Controller agrees to this Data Processing Agreement. For Enterprise clients requiring a signed DPA, please contact hello@daltonsai.com.
