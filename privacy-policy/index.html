Privacy Policy

Effective: 15 March 2026 · Version 2.0 · Daltons AI B.V.

1. Who We Are

Daltons AI B.V. (KVK 97688835) is based in Zaandam, Netherlands. We provide AI-powered lead scoring and automated outreach services for B2B companies. Contact: privacy@daltonsai.com

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Name, email, company, billing info when you subscribe.
  • Lead data: Names, emails, job titles, company info of prospects processed through our AI. This data is provided by you or sourced from publicly available business directories.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, interaction patterns.
  • Payment data: Processed securely by Stripe. We never store card numbers.

3. How We Use Your Data

To provide AI lead scoring and outreach services, process payments, send service communications, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR/AVG)

We process data based on: contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)), legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)), and consent where required. For lead data, clients act as data controllers; we act as data processor under our DPA.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with: Anthropic (AI scoring), Stripe (payments), Instantly.ai (email delivery), Supabase (EU-West database). We do not sell personal data.

6. Retention

Account data: subscription duration + 12 months. Lead scoring data: 6 months. You may request earlier deletion.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR: access, correction, deletion, restrict processing, data portability, and objection. Contact privacy@daltonsai.com.

8. Security

TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 at rest, role-based access controls, EU-West database hosting.

9. Cookies

Essential cookies only. No tracking or advertising cookies.
