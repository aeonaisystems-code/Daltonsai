Data Processing Agreement

Daltons AI B.V. · KVK 97688835 · Amsterdam, Netherlands

Version 1.0 Effective: 11 March 2026 Last updated: 11 March 2026 GDPR Article 28 Compliant

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") is entered into between Daltons AI B.V. as Processor and the subscribing Client as Controller. This DPA forms an integral part of the Daltons AI Terms of Service and is binding upon subscription to any Daltons AI plan.

This DPA is required by Article 28 of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) 2016/679 and the Dutch UAVG. It applies to all personal data processed by Daltons AI on behalf of the Client in connection with the delivery of AI Lead Autopilot, AI Market Intelligence and AI Deal Operations services.

Enterprise plan clients requiring a signed PDF copy of this DPA should contact legal@daltonsai.com.

01 Definitions

In this DPA, the following definitions apply:

  • "Controller" means the Client — the party that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.
  • "Processor" means Daltons AI B.V. — the party that processes personal data on behalf of the Controller under this DPA.
  • "Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person as defined in GDPR Article 4(1).
  • "Processing" means any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, as defined in GDPR Article 4(2).
  • "Data Subject" means an identified or identifiable natural person whose personal data is processed.
  • "Sub-processor" means any third party engaged by Daltons AI to process personal data under this DPA.
  • "Data Breach" means a breach of security leading to accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure of or access to personal data.
  • "SCCs" means Standard Contractual Clauses as adopted by the European Commission for international data transfers.
  • "Services" means the AI Lead Autopilot, AI Market Intelligence and AI Deal Operations services as described in the Terms of Service.

04 Processor Obligations (Daltons AI)

Daltons AI B.V. agrees and warrants to:

  • Process personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller, unless required to do so by Union or Member State law
  • Ensure all personnel authorised to process personal data are bound by enforceable confidentiality obligations
  • Implement and maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures pursuant to GDPR Article 32 (see Section 08)
  • Assist the Controller in fulfilling its obligations to respond to data subject rights requests (access, erasure, portability, etc.) within the timeframes required by applicable law
  • Assist the Controller in ensuring compliance with GDPR Articles 32–36 (security, breach notification, DPIAs, prior consultation)
  • Delete or return all personal data to the Controller upon termination of the agreement, and delete existing copies unless Union or Member State law requires storage
  • Make available to the Controller all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with GDPR Article 28 obligations and allow for audits conducted by the Controller or its authorised auditor
  • Notify the Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of a personal data breach involving Controller's data (see Section 09)
  • Maintain a record of all processing activities carried out on behalf of the Controller as required by GDPR Article 30(2)

05 Controller Obligations (Client)

The Controller agrees and warrants to:

  • Ensure there is a valid and documented lawful basis under GDPR Article 6 for all personal data provided to or processed through Daltons AI
  • Ensure any outreach conducted using leads generated through the platform complies with applicable anti-spam, marketing and privacy laws in the recipients' jurisdictions
  • Provide accurate, complete and timely instructions for data processing activities
  • Notify Daltons AI promptly of any data subject requests, regulatory inquiries or complaints relating to data processed through the platform
  • Comply with all applicable data protection laws in the Client's jurisdiction and target markets
  • Inform Daltons AI of any specific data localisation requirements applicable to the Controller's jurisdiction

07 International Data Transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), Daltons AI ensures appropriate safeguards are in place pursuant to GDPR Chapter V, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs): EU Commission Implementing Decision 2021/914 (Module 2: Controller to Processor, and Module 3: Processor to Processor) are incorporated by reference into contracts with all US-based sub-processors
  • Transfer Impact Assessments (TIAs): Completed for all US-based sub-processors to ensure an equivalent level of protection is maintained
  • Supplementary measures: Including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and pseudonymisation where technically feasible, implemented in accordance with EDPB Recommendations 01/2020
  • UK transfers: UK International Data Transfer Agreements (IDTAs) used for transfers to/from UK-based services where applicable post-Brexit

09 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach involving data processed under this DPA:

  • Daltons AI will notify the Controller within 48 hours of becoming aware of the breach — this is stricter than the GDPR 72-hour supervisory authority requirement to give the Controller time to assess and report
  • Notification will be sent to the email address registered on the Controller's account
  • Initial notification will include at minimum: the nature and extent of the breach, estimated number of data subjects affected, categories of data involved, and immediate containment measures taken
  • Daltons AI will provide a full incident report within 5 business days including root cause analysis and remediation measures
  • Daltons AI will cooperate fully with the Controller in complying with its own notification obligations to the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens and/or affected data subjects
  • Daltons AI will document all breaches regardless of whether they meet the threshold for notification to supervisory authorities

10 Data Subject Rights Assistance

Daltons AI will assist the Controller in responding to data subject rights requests under GDPR Articles 15–22. Specifically:

  • Client portal provides self-service tools to export all lead data in CSV format within 24 hours of request
  • Permanent deletion of any individual's data can be completed within 5 business days of a documented request from the Controller
  • Any data subject requests received directly by Daltons AI will be forwarded to the Controller immediately, with no direct response to the data subject except to acknowledge receipt
  • Full audit trail of all processing activities for a given data subject is available to Enterprise plan clients upon request

11 Audit Rights

The Controller has the right to conduct, or commission an independent auditor to conduct, an audit of Daltons AI's data processing activities and security measures. The following conditions apply:

  • Written notice of at least 30 days must be provided before any audit
  • Audits may be conducted no more than once per calendar year (unless triggered by a confirmed data breach)
  • The Controller bears the cost of any third-party auditor engaged
  • Daltons AI may fulfil audit obligations by providing current third-party security certifications or audit reports (e.g. SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001) where available
  • Audit activities must not unreasonably disrupt Daltons AI's business operations or compromise the security of other clients

12 Data Return & Deletion

Upon termination or expiry of the subscription, at the Controller's choice and within 30 days of termination:

  • Return: Daltons AI will provide a full export of all Client data in CSV format via the client portal or secure file transfer
  • Deletion: Daltons AI will delete all personal data processed on behalf of the Controller from its systems and from sub-processors' systems, with written confirmation of deletion provided within 10 business days
  • Daltons AI may retain data for longer only where required by applicable law (e.g. Dutch tax records retention), and will notify the Controller of any such retained data and its legal basis

In the event of any conflict between this DPA and the Terms of Service, the provisions of this DPA shall prevail with respect to the processing of personal data.
